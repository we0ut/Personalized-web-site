Education

DSU-RCSOO "Nikola Karev" - Strumica

(Semptember 1st 2020 - June 10th 2024)

During my time at DSU RCSOO "Nikola Karev" in Strumica, I gained a strong foundation in various areas of computer science and electrical engineering. The curriculum was diverse and hands-on, covering key subjects such as electrotechnics, programming in C++, web design, and computer networks and security.

The experience was both challenging and rewarding, and it played a crucial role in shaping my technical knowledge and preparing me for future academic and professional pursuits in the IT field.

Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering

(October 10th 2024 - Now)

I've just completed my first year of Computer Science at FINKI in Skopje, and it has been an enriching and challenging journey. Throughout the year, I studied core subjects such as Object-Oriented Programming, Calculus 1 and 2, Discrete Mathematics 1 and 2, Fundamentals of Web Design, and Computer Architecture and Organization.

These courses provided a solid theoretical and practical foundation in both software development and the underlying principles of computer systems. I developed strong problem-solving and programming skills, especially through hands-on coding assignments and projects. This first year has not only deepened my interest in computer science but also prepared me for more advanced topics in the years ahead.

Various Erasmus+ Projects

I have had the opportunity to participate in two Erasmus+ projects that greatly enriched my personal and professional development. The first project, InnoTheater, took place in Barcelona, Spain, and focused on using innovative theater methods to foster creativity, collaboration, and self-expression among young people. It was a unique and engaging experience that allowed me to develop my communication and teamwork skills in a multicultural environment.

The second project was held in Stockholm, Sweden, and was centered around renewable energy and sustainability. Through workshops, discussions, and hands-on activities, I gained deeper insight into green technologies and the importance of sustainable practices in today's world. Both projects were unforgettable and helped me grow as a globally minded and socially responsible individual.
