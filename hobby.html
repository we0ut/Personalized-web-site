My Hobbies

A Lifelong Passion for Guitar

Playing guitar has been one of the biggest passions in my life for the past seven years. I own two guitars—a Jackson JS32 and an Ibanez 7-string—that I use to explore a wide range of tones and styles, especially within the heavier and more progressive genres. I love both writing my own riffs and compositions, as well as learning songs from bands that have inspired me over the years. Some of my biggest influences include Periphery, Bring Me the Horizon, Trivium, Spiritbox, and Loathe—bands whose music has had a huge impact on my playing style and creativity.

I've had the incredible opportunity to see all of them live, which only deepened my appreciation for their musicianship and stage presence. Watching them perform gave me even more motivation to continue improving and expressing myself through music. Whether I'm jamming, experimenting with new ideas, or working on technique, the guitar is my go-to outlet and a key part of who I am.

My Passion for Traveling the World

Traveling is one of my greatest passions, and I truly hope that one day I'll have the chance to explore every corner of the world. There's something incredibly exciting and fulfilling about stepping into a new place—hearing a different language, trying unfamiliar food, and getting immersed in a culture completely different from your own. I've been lucky enough to visit a wide range of incredible cities, including Vienna, Barcelona, Bern, Stockholm, and Berlin, each offering its own unique charm, history, and atmosphere.

I've also explored much of the Balkans, which has helped me understand and appreciate the diversity and richness of the region I come from. Whether I'm walking through historic streets, admiring architecture, or simply meeting new people, every trip has left me with unforgettable experiences and valuable life lessons. Traveling has opened my mind, inspired my creativity, and deepened my appreciation for the world's beauty and complexity. It's a journey that never truly ends, and I hope to continue discovering new places, cultures, and perspectives for the rest of my life.

Why Video Games Will Always Be a Part of Me

Video games have been a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember, and my love for them has only grown stronger over the years. The first game I clearly remember playing was Need for Speed: Underground 2, and since then, I've been hooked on the excitement, creativity, and immersive experiences that gaming offers. What fascinates me most about video games is the ability to dive into rich, imaginative worlds and be a part of incredible stories—whether it's saving a futuristic city, fighting off enemies in epic battles, or simply exploring a vast open world full of mystery and wonder.

I enjoy playing all kinds of genres, including open-world adventures, action-packed shooters, hack and slash games, and story-driven titles. Some of my all-time favorites include the Metal Gear Solid franchise, Devil May Cry 5, Horizon Zero Dawn, and many others. These games have not only entertained me but also inspired me with their storytelling, characters, and gameplay mechanics. For me, gaming is more than just a hobby—it's a form of art and a way to experience emotions, challenges, and narratives in a deeply interactive way.
